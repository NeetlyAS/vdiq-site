About
The person behind VDiQ
photo
Steven van Dijk
Founder, CEO · European and Dutch Patent Attorney
After graduating in Electronic Engineering from Eindhoven University of Technology, Steven worked at Philips Research Laboratories as a Research Scientist, filing 24 invention disclosures. In 2003 he joined Philips IP&S as patent attorney trainee, qualified as Dutch patent attorney and began European Patent Attorney training. He later moved through NXP Semiconductors and DeltaPatents. In 2013 he emigrated with his family to Norway and joined Håmsø Patentbyrå in Stavanger — 7 years as senior attorney, nearly 5 as partner. At the end of 2024, he founded VDiQ IP Services.
